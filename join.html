Frequently Asked Questions

What is the membership fee?

The standard BNI annual membership fee varies by region. Please consult with the chapter leadership team for the current rate applicable to BNI AURA.

How often do we meet?

BNI AURA meets once every week for a highly structured 90-minute session. Attendance and punctuality are key to success.

Can I join if my category is taken?

No. BNI maintains exclusivity, meaning only one professional per business category is permitted in a chapter. If your category is taken, you may join the waiting list or explore other BNI chapters.

What is the time commitment?

The minimum commitment is attending the weekly meeting, plus regular 1-to-1 meetings with other members to deepen relationships.

How long before I see results?

Results depend on your participation. Members who fully embrace the "Givers Gain" philosophy often start seeing quality referrals within the first few months.

What if I want to visit first?

You are welcome to visit BNI AURA as a guest up to two times before submitting a membership application. Please use our 'Get Invited' page to register your visit.
